About Placements..

This Placement Management Portal is developed for students and administration of SKIT Jaipur to ensure a smooth and efficient placement process. Students can register themselves, check upcoming companies, and view placement statistics. Admins can manage student data, update company drives, and track placement records.

Inspired by our founder Swami Keshvanand Ji, our mission is to provide a platform that bridges the gap between students and top recruiters, ensuring transparency, readiness, and excellence in every placement season.
