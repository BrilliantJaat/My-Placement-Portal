ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING

About Electrical Engineering

Electrical Engineering (EE) is a branch of engineering that deals with the study, design, and application of electrical systems, equipment, and devices. It covers areas such as power generation, transmission, distribution, control systems, electronics, and renewable energy technologies.

Core Subjects

  • Electrical Circuits and Networks
  • Power Systems
  • Control Systems
  • Electrical Machines
  • Power Electronics
  • Renewable Energy Systems

Career Opportunities

Graduates in Electrical Engineering can work as Power Engineers, Electrical Design Engineers, Control Engineers, Project Engineers, and Energy Analysts. They find opportunities in industries such as power generation, manufacturing, renewable energy, automation, and government utilities.

Scope & Future

With the global shift towards clean and sustainable energy, Electrical Engineers are in high demand. Fields like solar power, wind energy, electric vehicles, and smart grids are creating new opportunities for skilled professionals.
