COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING

About Computer Science

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is a discipline that integrates computer engineering and computer science. It covers the design, development, and maintenance of software and hardware systems. Students learn about programming, algorithms, data structures, databases, operating systems, and artificial intelligence.

Core Subjects

  • Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python)
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Database Management Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Operating Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Career Opportunities

CSE graduates can work as Software Developers, Data Scientists, Network Engineers, AI Specialists, Cybersecurity Experts, and more. They find employment in top companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Infosys, TCS, and many startups.

Scope & Future

With the growth of technology, the demand for skilled computer engineers is increasing rapidly. Emerging fields like AI, Cloud Computing, and Quantum Computing are opening new possibilities.
